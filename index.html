You must be at least 18 years old and a resident of Nigeria (or eligible jurisdiction) to create an account. By registering, you confirm you meet these requirements.

4. Content standards

You must not post: hate speech, explicit sexual content, violence, harassment, misinformation, or content that violates Nigerian law. MeriSocial reserves the right to remove any content that violates these standards without prior notice. Repeated violations result in permanent account removal.

5. Creator handles and social growth

Submitted handles must be real accounts you own. Fake handles, purchased followers, or bot accounts are strictly prohibited. Use of automation tools or click farms results in permanent banning with no refund.

6. Creator earnings and payouts

Eligible creators receive monthly payouts from a revenue pool funded by platform advertising and promotional income. Eligibility requires: an active Premium subscription, a minimum of 50 Premium followers, 50,000+ impressions in the last 3 months, and zero active strikes. Payouts are calculated proportionally by engagement score. MeriSocial reserves the right to adjust eligibility criteria and pool size. Payouts are disbursed to your linked MeritWise wallet. MeriSocial is not liable for delays caused by wallet activation or third-party payment processors.

7. Subscriptions and payments

Subscriptions are non-refundable and do not auto-renew. When your plan expires, you must purchase a new subscription. Payments are processed securely by Paystack. MeriSocial does not store card details.
