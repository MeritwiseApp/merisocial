Terms and Community Rules

Active participation

Members must genuinely support the handles shared in each drop during their active subscription. Failure to engage is a violation of community standards.

Three-strike system

Three unresolved strikes result in permanent removal. Removed members forfeit remaining subscription time. No refunds.

Real handles only

You must submit real, active accounts you own. Submitting fake or others' handles results in an immediate strike.

No automation

Bots, scripts, and click farms are strictly prohibited and result in permanent banning.

Privacy

Your personal details are never shared. Only your handle and platform are visible, and only during a live drop window.

Subscriptions

No auto-renewal. When your plan expires you must purchase a new one. All sales are final.

Reinstatement

Removed members may appeal by contacting support. If reinstated, a new subscription at full price is required.
